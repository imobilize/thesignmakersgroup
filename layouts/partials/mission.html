Who We Are

The Signmakers Group is a UK-based holding company focused exclusively on the sign manufacturing sector.

We partner with talented business owners who have built profitable, well-run companies and want:

  • A secure exit
  • A succession solution
  • Capital for growth
  • Expertise and guidance
  • Or the strength of being part of something bigger

We preserve what makes each company great — its people, reputation and craftsmanship — while providing the structure, systems and capital to scale sustainably.

Our Vision: To build the most respected network of independent sign manufacturing businesses in the United Kingdom.

We believe the UK sign industry is fragmented and undervalued. Through strategic acquisitions and operational excellence, The Signmakers Group will create:

  • Economies of scale

  • Cross-regional collaboration

  • Stronger supplier relationships

  • Improved margins

  • Sustainable long-term growth
